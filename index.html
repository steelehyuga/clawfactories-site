What is ClawFactories

ClawFactories are installable agent skills for OpenClaw that automate book creation from concept to publish-ready package. Each factory targets a specific format — novels, puzzle books, coloring books — and produces everything Amazon KDP requires: manuscript files, cover art, descriptions, keywords, and pricing.

Built for people who want income from publishing without the months of manual work.

How it works

Install the factory

One command. The skill installs into your OpenClaw workspace and is ready to run.

Tell it what to publish

Pick a genre, set your preferences, and let the factory build your book — manuscript, covers, and all metadata.

Upload to Amazon

Take the finished package — DOCX, covers, description, keywords — and publish on KDP. That's it.
