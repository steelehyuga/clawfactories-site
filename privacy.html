Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

ClawFactories ("we", "us", "our") operates clawfactories.com. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you purchase a product, we collect:

  • Your email address (to deliver your purchase)
  • Your name (for billing purposes)
  • Payment information — processed and stored by Stripe, never by us

When you sign up for our email list, we collect your email address only.

We do not use cookies for tracking. We do not run ad pixels.

How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver products you have purchased
  • To send transactional emails (purchase confirmation, download links)
  • To send product update notifications if you opted in

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — file delivery. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Resend — transactional email. Resend Privacy Policy
  • Netlify — website hosting. Netlify Privacy Policy

Data Retention

We retain your email address and purchase records for as long as required for tax and accounting purposes (typically 7 years). You may request deletion of your email from our marketing list at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

Contact

For privacy-related requests: support@clawfactories.com
